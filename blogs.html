India and it’s magnificent cuisine
Just like our diversity in languages and culture ,the variety of food combinations and cuisines are yet another story to be told.From North to South or East to West we have delicious food to offer that tickles your taste buds.There's Goa's seafood, there's the wazwan of Kashmir and there's the coconutty richness of Kerala and the list goes on.Our traditional recipes have such abundance of spices that even today we can’t get over them.
Indian cuisine reflects an 8,000-year history of various groups and cultures interacting with the Indian subcontinent, leading to diversity of flavours and regional cuisines found in modern-day India. Later, trade with British and Portuguese influence added to the already diverse Indian cuisine.So the 1.3 billion population themselves have not tasted the diversity in its whole.Hence our platform wants to combine all of it for you to taste and ponder on which will be your next favourite.
